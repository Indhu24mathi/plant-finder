About The Project

Revolutionizing soil pollution management through technology and nature-based solutions

Smart Soil Pollution Management

Soil pollution is a major environmental problem affecting agriculture, food quality, and ecosystem health. Our proposed system monitors soil conditions using advanced pH, temperature, and moisture sensors to detect pollution levels in real-time.

An ESP-32 microcontroller collects and processes sensor data, transmitting it to a cloud platform for storage, monitoring, and analysis. A Machine Learning algorithm analyzes soil parameters and predicts pollution patterns, enabling early detection and prevention.

When abnormal soil conditions are detected, the system generates instant alerts to farmers or authorities, facilitating quick action and better management of soil pollution through phytoremediation plant selection.
